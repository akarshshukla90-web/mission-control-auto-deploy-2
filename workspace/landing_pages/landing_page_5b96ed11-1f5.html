Project Overview

Scrape data for JK Forge and Fitting project from the internet, focusing on relevant companies and contacts.

Create personalized emails for the scraped emails, ensuring a daily limit of 250 emails to avoid spam filters.

Develop a two-week plan to send these emails, with the goal of earning at least 50,000 INR from sales.

Two-Week Plan

Week 1: Lead Generation

Allocate 250 emails per day, targeting a total of 1,750 potential leads.

Week 2: Follow-up and Nurture

Continue with the same daily limit, focusing on follow-ups and nurturing the leads generated in the first week.

Performance Metrics

Track the email open rates, response rates, and conversion rates to refine the approach and improve sales outcomes.

Sales Goal

Aim to convert at least 2-3 leads per week into paying customers, with an average deal size of 8,333 INR to 12,500 INR.

This will require close collaboration with the sales team to ensure effective follow-ups and demos.

CRM Database Update

Update the CRM Database with the new leads and deals, and provide regular progress reports to Max.
