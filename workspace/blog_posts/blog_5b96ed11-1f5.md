### JK Forge & Fittings Email Marketing Strategy
#### Introduction
JK Forge & Fittings aims to leverage web scraping and personalized email marketing to boost sales. The objective is to earn at least 50,000 INR within a two-week period by targeting relevant companies and contacts.

#### Data Scraping and Email Personalization
Scrape data from the internet focusing on companies and contacts relevant to JK Forge & Fittings. Utilize this data to create personalized emails, ensuring a daily limit of 250 emails to avoid spam filters. This approach will help in establishing connections with potential leads without triggering spam detectors.

#### Two-Week Email Campaign Plan
- **Week 1:** Allocate 250 emails per day, targeting 1,750 potential leads. This initial outreach aims to introduce JK Forge & Fittings and spark interest in its products.
- **Week 2:** Continue with 250 emails per day, focusing on follow-ups and nurturing leads from Week 1. This phase is crucial for building relationships and converting leads into sales.

#### Performance Monitoring and Analysis
Track key metrics including email open rates, response rates, and conversion rates. Analyze these metrics to refine the email marketing strategy, improving its effectiveness and sales outcomes.

#### Sales Goal and Conversion Strategy
To achieve the sales goal of 50,000 INR, aim for 2-3 lead conversions per week, with an average deal size ranging from 8,333 INR to 12,500 INR. Close collaboration with the sales team is essential for effective follow-ups and demos, ensuring a smooth conversion process.

#### CRM Update and Progress Reports
Update the CRM database with new leads and deals. Provide Max with regular progress reports to ensure transparency and to make data-driven decisions for future campaigns.

By following this structured approach, JK Forge & Fittings can efficiently utilize scraped data, personalized emails, and strategic follow-ups to meet its sales objectives, enhancing its online presence and credibility through updated LinkedIn details and domain email communications.
