# World-Class Marketing Organization Hierarchy

## 1. Executive Leadership
- **Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)**: Marketing strategy, brand positioning, revenue contribution.
- **VP of Marketing**: Executes strategy, manages department heads.
- **Marketing Director**: Leads multiple marketing teams, tracks KPIs.

## 2. Brand Marketing
- **Brand Manager**: Identity, messaging, positioning, reputation.
- **Brand Strategist**: Long-term brand positioning.
- **Creative / Art Director**: Creative vision and visual execution.

## 3. Product Marketing
- **Product Marketing Manager (PMM)**: Positioning, GTM, sales enablement.
- **Go-to-Market (GTM) Manager**: Coordinates cross-functional launches.
- **Competitive Intelligence Analyst**: Tracks competitors and trends.

## 4. Growth Marketing
- **Growth Marketing Manager**: Acquisition, CRO, funnels.
- **Growth Analyst**: Measures acquisition, retention metrics.
- **Lifecycle / Referral Marketing Manager**: Onboarding, referral programs.

## 5. Performance Marketing
- **Performance Marketing Manager**: Paid advertising, ROI optimization.
- **PPC / Paid Social / Display Specialist**: Manages specific paid channels.

## 6. Search Marketing
- **SEO Manager**: Organic growth, technical SEO.
- **Technical / Local / ASO SEO Specialist**: Technical site architecture, local search, app stores.

## 7. Content Marketing
- **Content Marketing Manager**: Strategy, editorial calendar.
- **Content Strategist**: Themes and customer journeys.
- **Copywriter / Tech Writer / Blog Writer**: Content generation.
- **Email / Newsletter Specialist**: Email campaigns.

## 8. Social Media Marketing
- **Social Media Manager**: Platform strategy, community growth.
- **Community Manager / Influencer Marketing Manager**: Community building and partnerships.

## 9. Creative Team
- **Graphic / UI / Motion Designer**: Visual asset creation.
- **Video Editor / Videographer / Photographer**: Media production.

## 10. Public Relations (PR)
- **PR Manager**: Media relations, crisis communication.
- **Communications Manager**: Internal and external comms.

## 11. Events & Partnerships
- **Events / Sponsorship / Partnership Marketing Manager**: Events, co-marketing, sponsorships.

## 12. Customer Marketing
- **Customer Marketing Manager**: Case studies, advocacy.
- **Loyalty Program Manager**: Rewards programs.

## 13. Marketing Operations
- **Marketing Operations Manager**: Automation, CRM integration.
- **CRM / Automation Specialist**: Workflows and segmentation.

## 14. Marketing Analytics
- **Marketing Analyst**: Measures ROI, CAC, LTV.
- **Data / Attribution / BI Analyst**: Data analysis and dashboards.

## 15. Research
- **Market Research Analyst**: Sizing, trends, competitors.
- **Consumer Insights Manager / UX Researcher**: Usability and customer insights.

## 16. Web & Conversion
- **Web Marketing Manager**: Website performance.
- **CRO / Landing Page Specialist**: Experimentation and conversion optimization.

## 17. Emerging Marketing
- **AI Marketing Specialist / Prompt Engineer**: AI-powered workflows.
- **Marketing Technologist**: MarTech stack implementation.

---

# Marketing Workflow from Idea to Revenue
1. Conduct market and customer research
2. Define target audience and ideal customer profile
3. Develop brand strategy and positioning
4. Create messaging and value proposition
5. Plan go-to-market strategy
6. Produce creative assets and content
7. Build website and landing pages
8. Launch SEO and content initiatives
9. Execute paid advertising campaigns
10. Publish social media content
11. Run email marketing campaigns
12. Collaborate with sales on lead handoff
13. Analyze campaign performance
14. Optimize through testing and iteration
15. Nurture leads until conversion
16. Support customer onboarding and advocacy
17. Measure ROI and refine strategy
