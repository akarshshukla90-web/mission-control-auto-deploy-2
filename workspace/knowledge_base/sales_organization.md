# World-Class Sales Organization Hierarchy

## 1. Executive Leadership
- **Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)**: Owns sales, marketing, CS, revenue.
- **VP of Sales**: Strategy, quotas, processes.
- **Director of Sales**: Regional management, territory planning.

## 2. Sales Management
- **Regional Sales Manager**: Meets territory targets.
- **Sales Manager**: Pipeline reviews, coaching.
- **Team Lead**: Day-to-day guidance.

## 3. Sales Operations
- **Sales Operations Manager**: CRM, reporting, comp admin.
- **RevOps Manager**: Sales/Marketing/CS analytics, forecasting.
- **Sales Analyst**: Pipeline and win rate analysis.
- **CRM Administrator**: HubSpot/Salesforce management.

## 4. Business Development
- **BD Director / Manager**: Strategic partnerships, enterprise accounts.
- **BD Executive**: Outbound networking.

## 5. Prospecting Team
- **SDR / BDR**: Cold calls, emails, LinkedIn, lead qualification.
- **Lead Gen Specialist**: Research, enrichment.
- **Appointment Setter**: Books meetings.

## 6. Closing Team
- **Account Executive (AE)**: Demos, discovery, closing.
- **Enterprise / Strategic AE**: Complex sales cycles.
- **Key Account Manager**: Major client relationships.

## 7. Customer Success
- **CS Director**: Retention, renewals, growth.
- **CS Manager (CSM)**: Onboarding, health monitoring.
- **Renewal Manager**: Contract retention.

## 8. Sales Enablement
- **Sales Enablement Manager**: Training, playbooks.
- **Sales Coach**: Negotiation improvement.

## 9. Pre-Sales
- **Solutions Consultant / Engineer**: Technical demonstrations.
- **Sales Engineer**: Bridges tech and commercial gaps.

## 10. Partnerships
- **Channel Sales Manager**: Reseller networks.

## 11. Customer Support
- **Support Manager / Tech Support**: Issue resolution.

## 12. Marketing Collaboration
- **Demand Gen / Growth Manager**: Qualified leads.
- **Product Marketing**: Positioning, intel.

## 13. Revenue Intelligence
- **Pricing / Comp Analyst**: Strategies, commissions.

## 14. Sales Technology
- **Sales Automation Specialist**: Lead routing, sequences.

## 15. Legal & Commercial
- **Contract / Proposal Manager**: RFP responses, pricing terms.
