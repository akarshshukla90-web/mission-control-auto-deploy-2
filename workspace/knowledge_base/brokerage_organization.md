# Complete Organizational Structure of a Brokerage Company

## 1. Executive Leadership
- **CEO**: Own company vision
- **COO**: Operations
- **CTO**: Technology
- **CIO**: Internal technology
- **CISO**: Cybersecurity
- **CFO**: Finance
- **CRO**: Chief Risk Officer
- **CMO**: Marketing
- **Chief Product Officer**
- **Chief Compliance Officer**
- **Chief Legal Officer**
- **Chief Data Officer**
- **Chief Information Security Officer**
- **Chief AI Officer**

## 2. Trading Division
- **Head of Trading**: Order execution, Market routing, Liquidity, Exchange connectivity
- **Equity Trader**: Stock execution, Order management
- **Options Trader**: Options chains, Greeks, Volatility
- **Futures Trader**
- **Forex Trader**
- **Fixed Income Trader**
- **Cryptocurrency Trading Manager**
- **Smart Order Routing Engineer**: Creates algorithms that determine the best exchange for each order.
- **Execution Quality Analyst**: Measures Slippage, Fill rate, Best execution

## 3. Quantitative Team
- **Chief Quant Officer**
- **Quant Researcher**: Pricing, Trading, Execution models (Math, Stats, Python, C++)
- **Quant Developer**: Builds trading algorithms.
- **Quant Analyst**: Analyzes market data.

## 4. Risk Department
- **Chief Risk Officer**
- **Market Risk Analyst**: Portfolio risk, Exposure, VaR
- **Credit Risk Analyst**
- **Operational Risk Manager**
- **Fraud Risk Manager**
- **Margin Risk Specialist**: Margin calculations.

## 5. Compliance
- **Chief Compliance Officer**
- **AML Officer**: Anti Money Laundering
- **KYC Specialist**: Know Your Customer
- **Regulatory Reporting Analyst**
- **FINRA / SEC Compliance Manager**
- **Trade Surveillance Analyst**: Monitors suspicious trades.

## 6. Brokerage Operations
- **Brokerage Operations Manager**: Trade settlement, Clearing, Corporate actions
- **Clearing Operations Specialist**
- **Settlement / Reconciliation Analyst**
- **Securities Lending Manager**
- **Custody Operations**

## 7. Product Team
- Exactly like a software company: Chief Product Officer, Product Managers, Product Owners, UX Researchers, Business Analysts

## 8. Engineering
- **VP Engineering**: Frontend, Backend, Mobile, Cloud, DevOps, SRE, Platform Engineers, API Engineers, QA, Automation QA

## 9. AI Team
- AI Engineers, ML Engineers, Prompt Engineers, LLM Engineers, Recommendation Engineers, Fraud Detection Engineers, Trading AI Engineers, Financial AI Researchers

## 10. Market Data Team
- **Market Data Director**: NYSE feeds, NASDAQ feeds, OPRA options feed, Futures feeds
- **Market Data Engineer**
- **Feed Handler Engineer**: Processes millions of updates every second.
- **Real-Time Data Engineer / Data Quality Engineer**

## 11. Financial Engineering
- Pricing Models, Portfolio Analytics, Greeks Engine, Margin Engine, Risk Engine, Tax Engine, Dividend Engine, Corporate Actions Engine

## 12. Payments
- Payment Engineers, ACH Specialists, Wire Specialists, Credit Card Specialists, Treasury Operations

## 13. Customer Experience
- Customer Support, Premium Support, VIP Support, Trading Support, Technical Support, Account Opening Team, KYC Team

## 14. Sales
- Institutional Sales, Enterprise Sales, Broker Partnerships, Affiliate Team, Relationship Managers

## 15. Marketing
- Growth Marketing, SEO, Paid Ads, Brand, Influencer Marketing, Content, Social Media, PR, Email Marketing, Lifecycle Marketing, Referral Program Team

## 16. Legal
- Legal Counsel, Contract Specialists, Licensing, Intellectual Property, Regulatory Lawyers, Privacy Team

## 17. Security
- Security Engineers, SOC Analysts, Penetration Testers, Identity Engineers, Cloud Security Engineers, Fraud Engineers

## 18. Infrastructure
- Cloud Engineers, Network Engineers, Database Engineers, Storage Engineers, Observability Engineers, Reliability Engineers, Disaster Recovery Team

## 19. Data Team
- Data Engineers, Data Scientists, BI Analysts, Analytics Engineers, Data Warehouse Engineers, Streaming Engineers

## 20. Finance
- Controllers, Treasury, Accounting, Payroll, FP&A, Tax Specialists, Auditors

## 21. Human Resources
- Recruiters, HR Business Partners, Learning & Development, Compensation & Benefits, Talent Acquisition

## 22. Research Division
- Market Analysts, Economic Analysts, Equity Research Analysts, ETF Researchers, Macro Researchers, Portfolio Analysts, Investment Strategists

## 23. Business Intelligence
- Revenue Analysts, Customer Analytics, Product Analytics, Executive Dashboard Team

## 24. Infrastructure Operations
- NOC Engineers, Production Support, Release Engineers, Incident Managers, Site Reliability Engineers

## 25. Specialized Trading Technology
These roles are unique to top brokerages and are rarely found in ordinary software companies:
- Exchange Connectivity Engineer, FIX Protocol Engineer, Low-Latency Systems Engineer, FPGA Engineer, High-Frequency Networking Engineer, Matching Engine Engineer, OMS (Order Management System) Engineer, EMS (Execution Management System) Engineer, Smart Order Routing Engineer, Clearing Integration Engineer, Market Data Feed Engineer, Real-Time Pricing Engineer, Risk Engine Developer, Margin Engine Developer, Portfolio Analytics Engineer, Trade Surveillance Engineer, Algorithmic Trading Engineer, Quantitative Developer, Financial Data Engineer, Regulatory Reporting Engineer

## Knowledge Areas Required Across the Organization

### Finance
- Equity markets, ETFs, Mutual funds, Options, Futures, Forex, Bonds, Commodities
- Portfolio management, Margin trading, Securities lending, Clearing and settlement

### Regulations
- SEC regulations, FINRA rules, SIPC protection, AML, KYC, OFAC sanctions, PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001

### Technology
- Distributed systems, Low-latency networking, Cloud computing, Cybersecurity, Databases
- Streaming systems (Kafka, Pulsar), APIs, FIX protocol, WebSockets
- High-performance C++, Java, Rust, Go, Python

### AI & Data
- Machine Learning, Fraud detection, Risk modeling, Recommendation systems, Predictive analytics
- LLMs for customer support and research
