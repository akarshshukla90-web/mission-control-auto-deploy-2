**JK Forge & Fittings and JK Traders B2B Marketing Deliverable**
===========================================================

**Overview**
--------

JK Forge & Fittings and JK Traders are seeking to establish partnerships with suppliers of 2 inch schedule 80 elbow in Vadodara. As a senior B2B marketing strategist, our goal is to identify reliable suppliers, negotiate competitive pricing in INR, and provide a comprehensive list of sellers with detailed information.

**Target Audience**
-----------------

*   Oil & Gas companies
*   Refineries
*   EPC Contractors
*   Power Plants

These target buyers are likely to require high-quality, schedule 80 elbows for various industrial applications. Our strategy will focus on highlighting the benefits of partnering with JK Forge & Fittings and JK Traders.

**Strategy & Action Plan**
------------------------

### Step 1: Research and Supplier Identification

*   Utilize online directories like Thomasnet, Alibaba, and Tradekey to find suppliers of 2 inch schedule 80 elbows in Vadodara.
*   Search for local suppliers on Google Maps and other online platforms.
*   Contact local trade associations, such as the Vadodara Chamber of Commerce, to gather information about potential suppliers.

### Step 2: Shortlisting and Supplier Evaluation

*   Evaluate each supplier based on factors like product quality, pricing, delivery time, and customer service.
*   Shortlist 3-5 suppliers that meet our quality and pricing standards.

### Step 3: Price Negotiation and Comparison

*   Request quotes from the shortlisted suppliers, specifying the required quantity and delivery timeline.
*   Negotiate prices in INR to ensure competitiveness.
*   Compare prices among the shortlisted suppliers to determine the best option.

### Step 4: Supplier Verification and Due Diligence

*   Verify the suppliers' credentials, including their business registration, licenses, and certifications.
*   Assess their financial stability, creditworthiness, and reputation in the industry.
*   Evaluate their production capacity, quality control measures, and after-sales support.

**Key Messages/Copy**
-----------------

*   Emphasize the benefits of partnering with JK Forge & Fittings and JK Traders, such as:
    *   High-quality, schedule 80 elbows manufactured in Vadodara.
    *   Competitive pricing in INR.
    *   Reliable delivery times and excellent customer service.
    *   Expertise in carbon steel forged fittings and flanges.
*   Highlight the importance of working with local suppliers to reduce logistics costs and improve response times.

**Channels & Tactics**
-------------------

### Online Presence

*   Create a dedicated webpage for the 2 inch schedule 80 elbow product, featuring product specifications, pricing, and ordering information.
*   Utilize search engine optimization (SEO) techniques to improve the webpage's visibility on search engines.

### Email Marketing

*   Design and send targeted email campaigns to the shortlisted suppliers, introducing JK Forge & Fittings and JK Traders, and highlighting the benefits of partnering with us.

### Trade Shows and Industry Events

*   Participate in local trade shows and industry events to network with potential suppliers and promote our products.

### Referrals and Word-of-Mouth

*   Leverage existing relationships with EPC Contractors, Oil & Gas companies, and Refineries to gather referrals and recommendations.

**KPIs & Success Metrics**
-------------------------

*   Number of suppliers identified and shortlisted.
*   Price negotiation and comparison results.
*   Supplier verification and due diligence outcomes.
*   Lead generation and conversion rates.
*   Sales revenue and growth.

--------------

A comprehensive report detailing the following:

*   A comparison of prices among the shortlisted suppliers.

By following this comprehensive B2B marketing deliverable, JK Forge & Fittings and JK Traders can establish reliable partnerships with suppliers of 2 inch schedule 80 elbows in Vadodara, negotiate competitive pricing in INR, and increase sales revenue and growth.
